NIKI - NICOLE ZEFANYA

Singer, Songwriter, Producer

Nicole Zefanya (professional name NIKI) is an Indonesian singer-songwriter and producer born on 24 January 1999 in Jakarta. She rose to international attention through online covers and original uploads, later signing with the influential label 88rising.

NIKI's music blends alternative pop and R&B into cinematic, intimate songwriting that bridges Indonesian identity and global pop culture. Her career includes studio albums and singles that have placed her on major soundtracks and international charts.

With her distinctive voice and authentic storytelling, NIKI has become one of the most prominent Asian artists in the global music scene, inspiring a generation of fans with her artistry and vision.

NIKI's Profile

Nicole Zefanya, known professionally as NIKI, is an Indonesian singer-songwriter and producer born in Jakarta on January 24, 1999.

She gained attention through online covers and original song uploads, which later led her to sign with the influential label 88rising.

NIKI is known for her emotional vocal style and honest, personal songwriting. Her music often explores themes of love, identity, and life experiences as a young person.

In addition to her solo career, NIKI actively collaborates with various international artists and is part of the 88rising collective that promotes Asian artists on the global stage.

Career Journey

  • 2017: Started uploading covers and original songs on YouTube, gaining a significant following
  • 2018: Signed with 88rising and released debut single "I Like U"
  • 2019: Released EP "wanna take this downtown?" and performed at Head in the Clouds festival
  • 2020: Released debut album "MOONCHILD" to critical acclaim
  • 2022: Released album "Nicole" showcasing a more personal side
  • 2024: Released album "Buzz" and announced Buzz World Tour

NIKI's career journey shows consistent artistic evolution from an indie musician to an international pop star, while maintaining her authenticity and artistic integrity.

Achievements

  • Album "MOONCHILD" featured in various best albums of 2020 lists
  • Song "La La Lost You" reached over 100 million streams on Spotify
  • Named one of Forbes "30 Under 30 Asia"
  • Received platinum certification for several singles in Indonesia
  • Performed at Coachella as part of 88rising
  • Collaborations with artists like Rich Brian and Warren Hue received widespread praise
  • Her music videos have been viewed over 500 million times on YouTube

NIKI's achievements are not only measured in numbers but also in her influence in paving the way for Asian artists in the global music industry.
